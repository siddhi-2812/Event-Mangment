About The Festival Events

The Festival ceremony in Indian culture is a sacred and elaborate affair, often encompassing various rituals and traditions that vary based on regional customs and religious beliefs. It typically involves rituals such as the exchange of vows, blessings from elders, symbolic gestures, and the tying of the nuptial knot, signifying the union of two individuals and families.
